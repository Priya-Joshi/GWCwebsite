About This Map

My interactive map provides an insightful exploration into the global landscape of women's involvement in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. By hovering over different countries, I showcase the percentage of women holding STEM degrees, offering a revealing glimpse into gender representation in these vital sectors. I aim to spark conversations about progress and challenges in achieving gender equality in STEM around the world, highlighting both geographical variations and broader trends. My map is not just an informational tool, but a catalyst for deeper understanding and discussion about women's roles in science and technology globally. The information presented in the map range from 2012 - 2023.
